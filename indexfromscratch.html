Golden Doodles

Origin: Golden Doodles are a crossbreed between a Golden Retriever and a Poodle.

  • Temperament: Known for their friendly and intelligent nature.
  • Coat: They usually have a curly or wavy coat that can be low-shedding.

Training

Training: Golden Doodles are highly trainable and often excel in obedience training.

  • Exercise: Regular exercise is important for their physical and mental well-being.
  • Size: They come in various sizes depending on the Poodle parent (toy, miniature, or standard).
